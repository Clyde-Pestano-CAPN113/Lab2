What Are Hurricanes

hurricanes are powerful weather events that work by using the warm ocean water and moist, humid air. when the warm air is flowing upward at a zone with low pressure over warm ocean water, the water is released from the air creating the clouds for the strom. as it rises the air also roatates.

Step By Step On How Hurricanes Form

  1. Wind blows across warm ocean water
  2. the warm moist air rises
  3. as it goes up the air cools and is condensesd into large strom clouds.
  4. The cooling releases a lot of heat
  5. The heat transfer creates very strong winds
